# Mission: Survive the Cold

## Objective
Keep all 3 colonists alive and warm at -5°C for 3 in-game days. Wood is scarce (sparse trees). You must build enclosed, heated shelter immediately and manage wood as both building material AND fuel.

## Success Criteria
- All colonists alive at end
- No hypothermia health conditions
- Enclosed shelter with heat source built by end of day 1
- Indoor temperature above 10°C
- Nobody sleeps outside after day 1

## Strategy Priorities
1. **First 30 minutes**: chop ALL nearby trees — wood is life
2. Build a SMALL enclosed room (4x4) with campfire ASAP — not a full barracks
3. Campfire provides heat but burns wood — manage fuel supply
4. Build beds inside the heated room BEFORE nightfall
5. DO NOT over-build — every wood plank used for walls is one not burned for heat
6. Hunt deer for meat + leather (leather = warm clothes eventually)
7. Mine mountain corners for stone — stone walls don't consume wood
8. Transition from wood walls to stone walls once mining is established

## What Doesn't Matter
- Room impressiveness — ugly warm beats pretty cold
- Research — don't research until shelter is secure
- Beauty — irrelevant when you're freezing
- Multiple rooms — one warm room is enough for day 1
