# Quality System

Most crafted items have a quality level that affects their stats. Quality is determined by the crafter's skill at the moment of completion.

## Key Skill Thresholds

- **Skill 6**: break-even point (average value = material cost). Stop making awful items.
- **Skill 10**: majority Good+ (56.4% Good or better)
- **Skill 15**: 33.8% Excellent or better
- **Skill 20**: 60.1% Excellent+, 9.5% Masterwork

**Never craft quality items below skill 6** — average value is less than material cost.

## Legendary Items

**Legendary is NEVER possible through normal crafting.** Only via Inspired Creativity.

### Inspired Creativity
- Boosts quality by **2 levels** (Excellent → Legendary, Masterwork → Legendary)
- Duration: 8 days
- Minimum skill to receive: level 3
- Skill 20 + Inspired Creativity: **~60% chance of Legendary**
- Triggers: random (mood >= 50%), Tortured Artist trait, Word of Inspiration psycast
- Strategy: pre-start expensive items (grand sculpture, marine armor), assign inspired pawn to finish

## What Has Quality

- Furniture (beds, chairs, tables, dressers)
- Weapons (melee and ranged)
- Apparel (all clothing and armor)
- Art (sculptures)
- Some buildings (doors)

## What Does NOT Have Quality

- Walls, floors, stockpiles, zones
- Stone blocks, steel, components
- Raw food, meals (including packaged survival meals)
- Drugs, medicine
