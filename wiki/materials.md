# Materials (Stuff)

Materials determine the properties of walls, furniture, and other "stuffable" buildings. Choose based on the situation: speed, durability, beauty, or fireproofing.

| Plasteel | 448 | 1,870 | 100% | 25 Plasteel |

**Critical**: Stone doors open at 45% speed — causes traffic jams. Always use wood or steel doors even in stone buildings. Autodoors (post-Microelectronics research) eliminate the slowdown.

## When to Use What

- **Early game**: Wood for speed (walls in 95 ticks vs 950 for stone). Accept the fire risk.
- **Permanent base**: Stone walls (granite for defense, marble for beauty). Steel doors.
- **Impressive rooms**: Marble walls + marble furniture (1.35x beauty multiplier)
- **Defense**: Granite walls (510 HP, toughest stone). Steel or plasteel doors.
- **Accent pieces**: Gold or silver furniture in bedrooms/dining for wealth + beauty boost.
- **Never**: Stone doors (45% speed is crippling). Wood in killboxes (burns).
