# Killbox Design

A killbox funnels raiders through a narrow, heavily defended corridor. The most effective defense structure in RimWorld.

## Basic Anatomy

1. **Perimeter wall** around entire colony with **one open entrance**
2. **Approach corridor** (funnel) leading to the killing field
3. **Killing field** where enemies are exposed to concentrated fire
4. **Bunker** where colonists shoot from behind cover

## Recommended Dimensions

- Killing field: **12-24 tiles wide**, **18-24 tiles deep**
- Approach corridor: **1-2 tiles wide** with bends
- Funnel entry: **1 tile** (forces single-file)

## Design Variants

### Classic Killbox
- Half-circle with 1-tile entrance at center
- Colonists on curved side behind alternating walls and sandbags
- Turrets near entrance
- Fill the bend before entrance with barricades/sandbags to deny enemy cover

### Trap Tunnel
- Long winding 2-tile-wide passage
- Traps on one side, fences on other (colonists walk safely on fence side)
- Sandbags/chunks every other tile to slow movement
- Forces single-file through every trap

### Burn Box (Heat Trap)
- Stone-walled winding passage with doors held open
- Terminal room with wood floors and furniture
- Ignite with Molotov/incendiary launcher
- Superheated air kills organic raiders
- **Weakness**: mechanoids are immune to heat

### Singularity Killbox
- Compact, precise chamber design
- Open doors slow enemy advance
- Optimal shooting positions with brief fire windows
- Dedicated EMP grenade operator for mechs
- Most efficient design — handles nearly any raid type

## Key Principles

- **Bends break line of sight** — prevents raiders sniping back at you
- **Keep entrance OPEN** — raiders breach walls if no easy path exists
- **Obstacles in corridor** deny enemy cover while slowing them
- **Sappers route away from powered turrets** — use this to funnel them
- **All turrets go offline during solar flares** — have a backup plan

## Turret Placement

- Place on **sides** of killbox, not directly facing entrance
- Walls around back and sides of each turret
- **4+ tile spacing** between turrets (prevent chain explosion)
- Autocannons for medium range, slug turrets for long-range sniping

## Melee Blocking (Doorway Defense)

- **3 melee pawns** can attack 1 enemy in a doorway simultaneously
- Only 1 enemy fits in the doorway at a time → **3v1 advantage**
- Ranged colonists fire from behind the melee blockers
- Draft colonists block enemy movement
- Best melee weapons for blocking: longsword + shield belt

## Countering Raid Types

| Raid Type | Counter Strategy |
|-----------|----------------|
| Standard | Funnel through killbox |
| Sappers | IEDs behind outer walls, rocket launchers when bunched |
| Breachers | Mobile defense, plasteel/uranium walls at breach points |
| Drop pods | Indoor positions, can't land under overhead mountain |
| Siege | Rush early, or 2 incendiary mortars to force firefighting |
| Mechanoids | EMP grenades/mortars critical, high-AP weapons |

## Wall Materials for Defense

| Material | Wall HP | Best Use |
|----------|--------|----------|
| Wood | 195 | Never for perimeter (burns) |
| Granite | 510 | Best common perimeter wall |
| Uranium | 750 | Critical chokepoints |
| Plasteel | 840 | Best wall material overall |

Double-layer walls at chokepoints resist grenades and miniguns.
