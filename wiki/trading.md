# Trading

Trading is the primary way to acquire resources you can't produce. Social skill directly affects prices.

## Price Mechanics

- **Selling**: 60% of market value (you lose 40%)
- **Buying**: 140% of market value (you pay 40% extra)

### Item-Type Sell Multipliers

| Item Type | Multiplier | Effective Sell % |
|-----------|-----------|-----------------|
| Sculptures/Art | x1.1 | 66% |
| Furniture | x0.7 | 42% |
| Weapons | x0.4 | **24%** |
| Everything else | x1.0 | 60% |

**Never sell weapons** — only 24% return. Smelt them for materials instead.

### Trade Price Improvement (TPI)

Social skill improves trade rates at 1.0% per level:

| Social Level | TPI | You Sell At | You Buy At |
|-------------|-----|-----------|-----------|
| 0 | 0% | 60.0% | 140.0% |
| 5 | 5% | 63.0% | 133.0% |
| 10 | 10% | 66.0% | 126.0% |
| 15 | 15% | 69.0% | 119.0% |
| 20 | 20% | 72.0% | 112.0% |

### Additional TPI Sources
- Trading at faction settlement: +2%
- Inspired Trade inspiration: +18%

## Trade Infrastructure

### Orbital Trade Beacon
- Research: Microelectronics
- Cost: 40 Steel + 1 Component, 40W
- Radius: 15 tiles diameter
- Items within radius are available for sale
- Purchased items drop via pods to **unroofed** beacon tiles

### Comms Console
- Cost: 120 Steel + 4 Components, 200W
- Size: 3x2, Construction skill 5
- Required for orbital trading
- Place under roof (rain causes short circuit)

### Requirements for Orbital Trading
1. Powered comms console
2. Powered orbital trade beacon (at least one)
3. Orbital trader currently overhead (stays 16 hours)

- Arrives in ~2 days
- Military aid: 25 goodwill, 24-hour cooldown

## Most Profitable Trade Goods

### S-Tier
- **Flake** (80.46 silver/work-hr) — best overall trade good
- **Yayo** (72.92 silver/work-hr) — slightly less efficient than flake
- **Devilstrand clothing** at high quality — enormous value

### A-Tier
- **Beer** (69.56 silver/work-hr, includes fermentation)
- **High-quality dusters** — quality dramatically increases value
- **Corn** (163.5 silver/work-hr raw sale) — best raw crop

### B-Tier
- **Art/Sculptures** — every trader buys them (universal acceptance)
- **Smokeleaf joints** — easy to produce, decent value
- **Animal products** (wool, leather)

### Avoid
- **Weapons** (x0.4 sell multiplier)
- **Furniture** (x0.7 sell multiplier)
- **Penoxycyline** (barely profitable)

## Universal Trade Acceptance
All traders buy: Silver, Gold, Components, Insect jelly, Packaged survival meals.
