# Temperature and Weather

Temperature affects colonist comfort, crop growth, food spoilage, and heatstroke/hypothermia risk.

## Seasons and Temperature

RimWorld has 4 seasons of 15 days each (60-day year):
- **Spring** (1-15): warming, planting begins
- **Summer** (16-30): hottest, peak growing
- **Fall** (31-45): cooling, last harvest
- **Winter** (46-60): coldest, no outdoor growing

Temperature varies by biome. Temperate forest: ~-5C winter to ~30C summer.

## Crop Growing Temperatures

- **Minimum growing temp**: 10C (below this, plants stop growing)
- **Minimum survival temp**: 0C (below this, plants die)
- **Optimal range**: 10-42C
- Growing season is the period where outdoor temp stays above 10C

## Temperature Dangers

### Hypothermia
- Below 10C without warm clothing: hypothermia risk
- Below -10C: severe hypothermia, can kill
- Prevention: parkas, tuques, warm clothing. Also heated rooms.

### Heatstroke
- Above 40C: heatstroke risk
- Prevention: cowboy hats, dusters. Also cooled rooms.

## Indoor Temperature Control

### Heaters
- Raise room temperature
- Need power (conduit connection)
- One heater per ~25 tiles of room space

### Coolers
- Lower room temperature, vent heat outside
- **Placement**: must go IN a wall. Deconstruct wall first, then place cooler.
- Hot side faces outside, cold side faces inside
- Need power
- One cooler per ~16 tiles for freezer temps

### Campfires
- Raise temperature in small area (no power needed)
- Also serves as cooking station
- Uses wood as fuel

### Passive Cooler
- No power needed, uses wood
- Moderate cooling only
- Good for early game before electricity

## Freezer Design

- **Size**: 4x4 to 6x6 interior (small = easier to cool)
- **Double walls**: insulation reduces heat leak
- **Cooler count**: 1-2 coolers for a 4x4 freezer
- **Target temp**: -1C to 0C (frozen food lasts indefinitely)
- **Contents**: meals, raw meat, medicine, beer
- Never leave freezer door open (auto-doors help)

## Weather Effects

- **Rain**: extinguishes fires, causes outdoor work slowdown. Batteries explode if unroofed.
- **Snow**: slows movement, must be cleared from paths
- **Heat wave**: +20C above normal. Risk of heatstroke. Need coolers.
- **Cold snap**: -20C below normal. Crops die, hypothermia risk. Need heaters.
- **Thunderstorm**: lightning strikes can start fires. Prioritize firefighting.
- **Toxic fallout**: forces colonists indoors. Grow indoor crops or rely on stored food.
