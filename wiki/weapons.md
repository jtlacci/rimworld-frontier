Weapon choice and quality determine combat effectiveness. Material matters for melee; crafting skill matters for both.

### DPS Ranking (optimal range)
Chain shotgun (18.73) > Charge rifle (14.12) > Assault rifle (10.88) > Autopistol (7.69) > Revolver (6.32) > Bolt-action (5.63) > Sniper (5.00) > Greatbow (4.86) > Short bow (3.67)

### When to Use What
- **Sniper rifle**: long-range engagements, picking off enemies before they close
- **Assault rifle**: best general-purpose weapon (good DPS + decent range)
- **Chain shotgun**: devastating at close range, useless at long range
- **Charge rifle**: best all-around if you can afford plasteel
- **Bolt-action**: early-mid game ranged backbone

| Weapon | DPS | Cooldown | Damage Type | AP | Materials |
|--------|-----|----------|-------------|-----|-----------|
| Gladius | 3.53 | 2.0s | Sharp | 21% | 50 stuff |
| Mace | 3.48 | 2.0s | Blunt | 21% | 50 stuff |
| Longsword | 3.22 | 2.45s | Sharp | 24% | 100 stuff |
| Spear | 3.03 | 2.6s | Sharp | 42% | 75 stuff |

### Material Modifiers
- **Uranium**: x2.0 blunt damage, x0.6 sharp — best for maces/warhammers
- **Plasteel**: x0.6 blunt, x1.0 sharp — best for longswords/gladii
- **Jade**: high beauty, moderate damage
- **Wood**: lowest stats, cheapest

### Melee Strategy
- Gladius is highest DPS for melee
- Mace is best vs armored targets (blunt bypasses sharp armor)
- Spear has best armor penetration (42%)
- Use melee pawns for doorway blocking (1 pawn in 1-wide doorway blocks all enemies)
